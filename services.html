Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between architecture and interior design services?

+

Architecture deals with the structure and layout of a building, while interior design focuses on styling and functionality of interior spaces. At Bright Erection Studio, we integrate both for a complete solution.

Do you provide services for both residential and commercial projects?

+

Yes, we specialize in both. From homes and offices to retail and hospitality, we design for every need.

How much do your services cost in Mumbai?

+

Costs vary depending on the scope, size, and complexity of the project. We offer transparent pricing after an initial consultation.

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Residential projects typically take 3-6 months, while commercial projects may take 6-12 months from concept to completion.
